Green spinach smoothie

    Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup yoghurt (curd)
  • 1 pear
  • 2 tablespoon coconut

    continued...

  • 2 cup spinach
  • 15 green grapes
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice

Preparation method

  1. Wash spinach leaves with cold water. Chop them in small leaves.

  2. Peel the pear with the help of a peeler. Cut them into small slices.

  3. Blend curd, spinach, pear and green grapes together until thick and creamy.

  4. Strain the beverage. Serve.
