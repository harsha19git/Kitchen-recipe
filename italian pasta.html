Italian pasta

    Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rotelle or spiral pasta
  • 2-1/2 cups assorted cut-up fresh vegetables
  • 1/4 cup of broccoli
  • 1/4 cup of carrots
  • 1/4 cup of tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of bell peppers

    Continued...

  • 1/4 cup of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup of mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cubed cheddar or mozzarella cheese
  • 1/3 cup sliced pitted ripe olives (optional)
  • 1 cup Italian Dressing

Preparation method

  1. Boil the pasta till it is cooked and drain the remaining water.

  2. Combine all ingredients except Italian Dressing in large bowl. Add Dressing; toss well. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

  3. If preparing a day ahead, refrigerate, then stir in 1/4 cup additional Wish-Bone Dressing before serving.

  4. For a Creamy Italian Pasta Salad, substitute 1/2 cup real Mayonnaise for 1/2 cup Italian Dressing.
