Mixed fruit smoothie

    Ingredients

  • 2 banana
  • 2 guava
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 3 tablespoon honey

    Continued...

  • 14 strawberry
  • 3 cup orange juice
  • 3 sprigs mint leaves
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice

Preparation method

  1. Peel and cut banana into roundels. Hull the strawberries. Put the bananas and strawberries into the smoothie maker.

  2. Remove the seeds and chop the guava and put it the smoothie maker along with the orange juice. Blend well.

  3. Add salt, torn mint leaves and honey. Blend again.

  4. Add some ice cubes and blend again till the ice gets crushed. Lastly, add some lemon juice and sugar and blend again. Pour the smoothie into individual glasses.

  5. Use a strawberry to garnish and serve chilled.
